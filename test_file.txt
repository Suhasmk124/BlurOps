1. CORPORATE HISTORY AND OVERVIEW
ZephyrTech Solutions (formerly known as SolarWinds Enterprise Computing) was founded in 2012 with a primary focus on decentralized data management and encrypted network topographies. Over the last decade, the company has expanded its operational footprint across three continents, boasting a workforce of over 4,500 dedicated professionals. Our core mission has always been to ensure the absolute integrity of client data while maintaining lightning-fast retrieval speeds.

In 2018, ZephyrTech acquired Quantum Logic, bringing their proprietary vector-indexing algorithms in-house. This acquisition allowed the company to pivot towards AI-driven infrastructure, setting the stage for the Project Zeus initiative. The company’s revenue has grown consistently at a rate of 14% year-over-year, weathering market fluctuations through strict fiscal discipline and aggressive reinvestment in cybersecurity protocols. As we look toward the 2026 fiscal year, our primary objective is mitigating internal data leaks and securing our localized Large Language Models against prompt-injection attacks and vector-space reverse engineering.

However, recent audits have revealed that if an attacker gains access to the raw embedding vectors, they could potentially map the distances between tokens and reconstruct the original text.
